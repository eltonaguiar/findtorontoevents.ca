Take a deep breath. Notice how you feel now compared to when you started. You've successfully grounded yourself in the present moment.

Remember: You can use this technique anytime you feel overwhelmed.

Why 5-4-3-2-1 Works

This technique works by redirecting your attention from anxious thoughts to your immediate environment. By engaging all five senses, you activate different parts of your brain and interrupt the anxiety cycle.

Best used for: Panic attacks, anxiety spirals, dissociation, flashbacks, or whenever you feel disconnected from the present.
